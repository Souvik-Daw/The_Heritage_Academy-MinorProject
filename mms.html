Eligibility

 

Eligibility Norms set by the University

  • The applicant must have passed the Higher Secondary Examination of the West Bengal Council of Higher Secondary Education or its equivalent examination from a recognized Board / Council.
  • Candidate applying for admission to MSc in Media Science should be Graduate with 50% above marks in any stream.
  • Candidate has to appear for a Creative Test.

    • Common Admission Tests (CET)

  • Admission to Non-AICTE professional courses (UG, PG) of MAKAUT, WB is made through Common Entrance Test (CET) conducted by the University.
